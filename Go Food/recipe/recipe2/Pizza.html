Pizza

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F or 45°C)
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, onions, etc. (optional)
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)
First Step!
Make the Dough
Activate Yeast: In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until foamy. Mix Dough: In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until combined. Knead: Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Rise: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
Second Step!
Prepare the Sauce
In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 1 minute). Add tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Third Step!
Assemble the Pizza
Preheat Oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll Out Dough: On a floured surface, roll out the dough to your desired thickness. Add Sauce: Spread a layer of sauce over the dough. Add Toppings: Sprinkle with mozzarella and add any other toppings you like.
Fourth Step!
Bake
Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly
Enjoy!!
