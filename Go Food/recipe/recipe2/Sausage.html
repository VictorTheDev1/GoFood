Sausage

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • 1 pound (450g) ground pork
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for heat)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (optional for sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon oil (for frying)
First Step!
Prepare the mixture:
In a mixing bowl, combine ground pork, salt, pepper, sage, garlic powder, onion powder, red pepper flakes, and maple syrup (if using). Mix everything together thoroughly using your hands or a spoon until well incorporated.
Second Step!
Shape the patties:
Form the sausage mixture into small patties, about 2-3 inches (5-7 cm) wide and 1/2 inch (1 cm) thick.
Third Step!
Cook the sausage:
Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sausage patties and cook for 4-5 minutes per side or until they are golden brown and fully cooked (internal temperature should reach 160°F or 71°C).
Fourth Step!
Drain and serve:
Place the cooked patties on paper towels to drain any excess oil. Serve warm with eggs, toast, or your favorite breakfast sides.
Fifth Step!
Enjoy!!
