Chicken Burger

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • 2 chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar or American)
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • 1 red onion (sliced)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup, or your favorite sauce
First Step!
Prepare the Chicken Patty:
Season the chicken breasts with minced garlic, paprika, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook the chicken breasts for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature 165°F or 75°C). Optionally, flatten the chicken slightly for even cooking.
Second Step!
Toast the Buns:
Lightly toast the burger buns in a pan or toaster until golden brown.
Third Step!
Assemble the Burger:
Spread mayonnaise, ketchup, or your favorite sauce on the bottom bun. Add lettuce, a slice of tomato, and some onion rings. Place the chicken patty on top and add a slice of cheese. Top with pickles (if desired) and cover with the top bun
Fourth Step!
Serve:
Serve immediately with a side of fries, chips, or a fresh salad.
Enjoy!!
