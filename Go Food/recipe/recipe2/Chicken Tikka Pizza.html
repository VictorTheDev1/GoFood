Chicken Tikka Pizza

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • 1 store-bought or homemade pizza dough (about 12 inches)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for brushing)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • /2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded (optional for extra flavor)
First Step!
Marinate and Cook the Chicken Tikka
In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, oil, and salt. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, mix well, and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or 2 hours for best results). Heat a grill pan or skillet on medium heat, and cook the chicken until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes on each side.
Second Step!
Prepare the Pizza Base
Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F). Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to a 12-inch circle. Brush the base with olive oil.
Third Step!
Assemble the Pizza
Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough. Sprinkle half the mozzarella cheese. Add the cooked chicken tikka pieces, bell pepper slices, and onion slices. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese and cheddar cheese.
Fourth Step!
Bake the Pizza
Place the pizza on a baking tray or pizza stone. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
Fifth Step!
Garnish and Serve
Garnish with fresh cilantro and a sprinkle of red chili flakes. Slice and serve hot!
Enjoy!!
