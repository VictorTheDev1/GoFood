Meat Pie

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter (cut into cubes)
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 2 medium potatoes (diced)
  • 1 medium carrot (diced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon flour (for thickening)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 seasoning cube
First Step!
Prepare the filling: In a pan, heat the vegetable oil. Add the onions and sauté until soft. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
Second Step!
Add vegetables: Add the diced potatoes, carrots, seasoning cube, salt, and pepper. Stir well and cook for 5 minutes. Add the flour and water to thicken the filling. Let it simmer until the potatoes are tender. Set aside to cool.
Third Step!
Make the dough: In a bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the cold butter and rub it into the flour until it resembles breadcrumbs. Gradually add cold water and mix to form a dough. Wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
Fourth Step!
Roll out dough: Roll out the dough on a floured surface and cut out circles. Place some filling in the center of each circle.
Fifth Step!
Seal the pies: Fold the dough over the filling to form a half-moon. Seal the edges with a fork and brush with egg wash.
Sixth Step!
Bake: Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Place the pies on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
Enjoy!!
