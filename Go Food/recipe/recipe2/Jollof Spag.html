Jollof Spag

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • 1 pack (500g) spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large tomatoes (blended)
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 4 red bell peppers (blended)
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers (Ata Rodo)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water (for cooking)
First Step!
Cook the spaghetti: Boil the spaghetti in salted water until almost cooked. Drain and set aside.
Second Step!
Prepare the sauce: In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil. Add the chopped onions and garlic. Saut√© until fragrant.
Third Step!
Add the pepper mix: Add the blended tomatoes, bell peppers, and scotch bonnet peppers. Stir well and cook for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
Fourth Step!
Add seasonings: Add the tomato paste, thyme, curry powder, and seasoning cubes. Stir to combine and let it cook for another 5 minutes.
Fifth Step!
Combine spaghetti and sauce: Add the drained spaghetti to the sauce. Mix thoroughly to coat the spaghetti with the sauce.
Sixth Step!
Finish cooking: Cover the pot and let it cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.
Enjoy!!
