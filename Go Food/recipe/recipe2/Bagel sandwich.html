Bagel Sandwich

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • Bagel (plain, sesame, everything, or your favorite flavor)
  • Meat (2 slices of turkey, ham, chicken, or a vegetarian alternative)
  • Cheese (1-2 slices of cheddar, Swiss, or provolone)
  • Egg (optional, for a breakfast version)
  • Tomato (1-2 slices)
  • Lettuce or Baby Spinach (a handful)
  • Avocado (2-3 slices, optional)
  • Condiments (cream cheese, mayonnaise, or mustard)
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)
First Step!
Prepare the Ingredients
Slice the Bagel in half horizontally. Prepare your toppings: Wash and slice the tomato. Rinse the lettuce or spinach. If using avocado, peel and slice it
Second Step!
Toast the Bagel
Place the bagel halves in a toaster or toaster oven. Toast until golden and slightly crispy (about 2-3 minutes).
Third Step!
Cook the Egg (Optional)
If you want an egg in your sandwich: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Crack 1 egg into the skillet. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper. Fry the egg for about 2-3 minutes until the whites are set. (You can flip it if you want a fully cooked yolk.) Remove the egg and set it aside.
Fourth Step!
Add Condiments
Take the toasted bagel halves. Spread your favorite condiment on each half: Cream cheese, mayonnaise, or mustard.
Fifth Step!
Assemble the Sandwich
On the bottom half of the bagel, layer the ingredients in this order: 2 slices of meat (turkey, ham, chicken, etc.) 1-2 slices of cheese The fried egg (if using) 1-2 slices of tomato A handful of lettuce or baby spinach 2-3 slices of avocado (optional) Sprinkle a little salt and pepper for added flavor.
Sixth Step!
Close the Sandwich
Place the top half of the bagel on the sandwich.
Seventh Step!
Serve and Enjoy! Slice the sandwich in half for easier handling if you prefer. Enjoy your fresh, hearty, and delicious Bagel Sandwich!
Enjoy!!
