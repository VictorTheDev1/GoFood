Ofada Stew

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • 4 large green bell peppers (Tatashe)
  • 6-8 green scotch bonnet peppers (Ata Rodo)
  • 2 cups palm oil
  • 1-2 pounds assorted meat (beef, goat meat, offal)
  • 1 cup iru (fermented locust beans)
  • 2 medium-sized onions (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Seasoning cubes (to taste)
First Step!
Prepare the peppers: Blend the green bell peppers and scotch bonnet peppers to a coarse texture. Set aside.
Second Step!
Boil the meat: Season the assorted meat with salt, onions, and seasoning cubes. Boil until tender, then fry or grill the meat for extra flavor. Set aside.
Third Step!
Bleach the palm oil: Heat the palm oil in a pot over medium heat until it turns clear (about 10 minutes). Turn off the heat and let it cool slightly.
Fourth Step!
Add onions and iru: Once the oil has cooled, add the chopped onions and iru. Fry until the onions are translucent and fragrant.
Fifth Step!
Cook the pepper mixture: Add the blended pepper mix to the pot. Stir well and allow it to cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
Sixth Step!
Add meat and crayfish: Add the fried meat and ground crayfish to the pot. Stir to combine, and let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
Seventh Step!
Season to taste: Add salt and seasoning cubes as needed. Allow the stew to cook until the oil floats to the top.
Enjoy!!
