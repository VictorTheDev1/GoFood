Puff Puff

How To Make?

Ingredents Needed

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water (adjust as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying (vegetable or canola oil)
First Step!
Mix the dry ingredients:
In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, instant yeast, salt, and nutmeg (if using).
Second Step!
Add water:
Gradually add warm water to the dry ingredients, mixing until the batter is smooth and stretchy but not too runny. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter.
Third Step!
Rest the batter:
Cover the bowl with a cloth or plastic wrap and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it has doubled in size. This allows the yeast to activate.
Fourth Step!
Heat oil:
In a deep pan or pot, heat oil over medium heat until it reaches about 350°F (175°C). You can test the oil by dropping a tiny bit of batter in; if it sizzles and rises to the surface, the oil is ready.
Fifth Step!
Fry the puff puff:
Use your hand or a spoon to scoop small amounts of batter and carefully drop them into the hot oil. Fry in batches to avoid overcrowding.
Sixth Step!
Drain excess oil:
Remove the puff puff with a slotted spoon and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil.
Enjoy!!
